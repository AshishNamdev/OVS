INSTRUCTIONS FOR ON LINE VOTING

VOTING CRITIRIA IN INDIA

1. Citizen of India having attained 18 years of age.
2. His/her name must appear in the Voting List of Assembly/ Parliament elections, as the case may be.
3. He/she must have a Voters Identity Card. In case Voter I-Card is not there, he/she can show a proof of identity acceptable to the returning officer.

LOGIN PAGE

USER NAME:user must fill his/her full name as mentioned in voter ID.
PASSWORD:1;write your appropriate passward for login (min 6 character long).
2:candidate must enter passward according to the format.
3:passward must be a combination of digits,specil characters and alphabates.
RESET:you can reset or clear all the fields using reset button.
SIGN IN:finally sign in to submit your vote.

REGISTRATION FORM

NAME:fill your full name as mentioned in your voter ID.
BIRTHDATE:enter your birthdate in the formate specified in the same way as mentioned in your voting ID.
USER NAME:fill your full name with middle and last name.
PASSWORD:enter your login passward.
CONFIRM YOUR PSSWORD:re-enter your password for confirmation.
E-MAIL:enter your login password for further correspondance.
